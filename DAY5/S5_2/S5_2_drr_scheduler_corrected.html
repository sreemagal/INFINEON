Deficit Round Robin (DRR) Scheduler

Corrected implementation — quantum added on every fresh queue visit

Correct DRR rule: On every fresh arrival at a queue, add the full quantum to the deficit counter first. Then send frames one by one while deficit ≥ next frame size. When deficit is insufficient (or queue empties), rotate to the next queue carrying the remaining deficit forward. An empty queue has its deficit reset to zero.
