Weighted Round Robin (WRR) Scheduler

Each queue is served a fixed number of frames per round, proportional to its weight

WRR rule: At the start of each round, every queue is loaded with credit equal to its weight. The scheduler visits each queue in order and serves one frame per credit unit. When a queue's credit reaches zero it waits until the next round. Assumes all frames are the same size — see DRR for byte-fair scheduling with variable frame sizes.
