ATS — Asynchronous Traffic Shaping

IEEE 802.1Qcr · Interleaved Regulator — per-stream eligibility time enforcement

Core principle: Each stream has its own eligibility time — the earliest moment its next frame may be forwarded. A frame that arrives before its eligibility time is held back for that stream only. Frames from all other streams continue unimpeded. This is what makes it interleaved — per-stream hold without blocking other streams.
Eligibility time update (after forwarding each frame):
new_eligibility = max(arrival_time, old_eligibility) + L/r

L = frame length in bits  ·  r = committed rate in bit/s  ·  L/r = minimum inter-frame gap
